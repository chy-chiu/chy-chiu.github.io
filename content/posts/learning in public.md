Tonnes of people have written about learning in public. Here are my thoughts on it.

Pros:
- You are forced to organize your thoughts and write properly - it's a good exercise to get better at writing
- Demonstration of domain expertise to people who you might want to impress in the future

Cons: 
- If nobody reads what you write, you are sad
- If someone is reading what you write, they now know how little you know and how misinformed you are 
- When you stop maintaining your digital garden, it becomes extra embarrassing
